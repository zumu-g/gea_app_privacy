GEA App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Introduction

GEA ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("GEA App" or "the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Calendar Access: With your permission, the App can add property inspection times and auction dates to your device calendar. We do not read or store your existing calendar data.
  • Contact Actions: When you choose to call, SMS, or email a real estate agent, your device's native apps handle these actions. We do not store these communications.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Property Data: The App retrieves property listings from Grant Estate Agents via the VaultRE API. This data includes property details, photos, and agent contact information.
  • No Personal Tracking: We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, location data, or device identifiers.

How We Use Information

  • To display property listings from Grant Estate Agents
  • To allow you to save inspection times to your calendar (with your permission)
  • To enable you to contact agents about properties

Data Storage and Security

  • The App does not store personal data on external servers
  • Property data is cached locally on your device for performance
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required

Third-Party Services

The App uses the VaultRE API to retrieve property listing data from Grant Estate Agents. No personal information is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny or revoke calendar access in your device Settings
  • Delete the App at any time to remove all locally cached data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
