GEA App Support

Your guide to using the Grant's Estate Agents app

About the App

The GEA app is the official mobile application from Grant's Estate Agents, designed to help you find your perfect property in Adelaide and South Australia.

With our app, you can:

  • Browse properties for sale and rent in real-time
  • View open home schedules and inspection times
  • Save your favourite properties
  • Contact agents directly via call, SMS, or email
  • Get instant answers with our AI assistant "Ask Grants"
  • Add auctions and inspections to your calendar

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I save a property?

Tap the heart icon on any property card or property detail page to save it to your favourites. Access your saved properties from the Saved tab.

Q: How do I contact an agent?

On any property detail page, tap the "Book" or "Contact" button to see options for calling, sending an SMS, or emailing the listing agent directly.

Q: How do I find open homes near me?

Navigate to the "Open Homes" tab to see all upcoming inspections. You can filter by date to find open homes that suit your schedule.

Q: How do I add an inspection to my calendar?

On a property with a scheduled open home, tap "Add to Calendar" to save the inspection time to your preferred calendar app (Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.).

Q: What is "Ask Grants"?

"Ask Grants" is our AI-powered assistant that can answer questions about properties, provide market insights, and help you with your property search. Find it in the app's chat feature.

Q: Why aren't properties loading?

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try pulling down to refresh the property list. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app.

Q: How do I sign in with Apple?

Tap "Sign In" from the Profile tab, then tap "Sign in with Apple". Follow the prompts to authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

Troubleshooting

App not loading content

If the app appears blank or content isn't loading:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down on the screen to refresh
  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store

Sign in issues

If you're having trouble signing in:

  • Ensure you have a valid Apple ID configured on your device
  • Check that Face ID/Touch ID is enabled if prompted
  • Try using a different sign-in method (email/password)

Notifications not working

To receive property alerts:

  • Go to Settings > GEA on your device
  • Enable "Allow Notifications"
  • In the app, go to Profile > Notification Settings and enable your preferred alerts
